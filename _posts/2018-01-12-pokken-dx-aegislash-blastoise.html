In the Nintendo Direct Mini today we got to learn about a new Battle pack for Pokken Tournament DX featuring two new Battle Pokémon and four new Support Pokémon available for purchase right now in the Nintendo eShop on Nintendo Switch.

{% include image.html image="pokken-dx-aegislash" caption="Battle Pokémon Aegislash & Support Pokémon Mega Rayquaza and Mimikyu" %}

Wave 1 of the Battle Pack contains Battle Pokémon Aegislash (Steel/Ghost - Gen 6) and a new Support Pokémon Set featuring Mega Rayquaza (Dragon/Flying - Gen 3) and Mimikyu (Ghost/Fairy - Gen 7). Aegislash can change between two formes, his Blade Forme and Shield Forme, and players can wield them both. This wave is scheduled to be released on January 31st.

{% include image.html image="pokken-dx-blastoise" caption="Battle Pokémon Blastoise & Support Pokémon Mew and Celebi" %}

Wave 2 includes the new Battle Pokémon Blastoise (Water - Gen 1) and a new Support Pokémon Set featuring Mew (Psychic - Gen 1) and Celebi (Psychic/Grass - Gen 2). Blastoise is the second generation one start evolution to join the game alongside Charizard, and the seventh starter evolution overall. Wave 2 is set to be available on March 23rd.

Aegislash and Blastoise join the other 21 Pokémon already playabale in the Nintendo Switch version of Pokkén Tournament:
