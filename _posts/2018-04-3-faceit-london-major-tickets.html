FACEIT have announced the prices and tiers of tickets available for their Counter-Strike major tournament on September 20-23 live from the SSE Arena in Wembley.

Three tiers of pricing are available for fans who want to be at the centre of the action, including VIP, Premium and Standard tickets. Fans wanting a VIP experience can purchase tickets for £150, which will include VIP seating, access to the VIP Lounge and a limited edition goodybag containing exclusive swag. Premium ticket holders will also receive a premium goodybag and premium seating for £75. Both VIP and Premium ticket holders will be treated to exclusive FACEIT London Major merchandise. General admission or day tickets start at £15 for Thursday and Friday and £25 for Saturday and Sunday.

Your basic ticket options are:

  • GA Thursday OR Friday Single Day Pass - £15.00 (approx. $30 NZD or $22 USD)
  • GA Saturday OR Sunday Single Day Pass - £22.50 (approx. $44 NZD or $32 USD)
  • GA 4-day Pass - £52.50 (approx. $110 NZD or $75 USD)
  • Premium Weekend Pass - £75 (approx. $150 NZD or $110 USD)
  • VIP 4-day Pass - £150 (approx. $300 NZD or $210 USD)
