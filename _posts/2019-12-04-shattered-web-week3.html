"A Day At The Office" - It is that time of the week and the Shattered Web Operation has rolled into it's new week. This means there is 6 new challenges that have been revealed and for those of you with the Operation Pass, a bunch of new unlocks are possible.

Shattered Web Week 3 Challenges:

  • Punching in (1 Stars): Get 10 kills in Flying Scoutsman
  • File It Away (1 Stars): Spend 5,000 cash in a single Danger Zone match
  • Clerical Error (2 Stars): Get 30 kills in Guardian: Office
  • Hostile Takeover (2 Stars): Get 10 round wins in Competitive: Office
  • All in a Day's Work (3 Stars): Get 16 round wins in Wingman: Vertigo
  • Punching out (3 Stars): Get 5 hostage rescues, or kill 5 hostage rescuers in Casual: Hostage Group

    • Good luck in your Shattered Web challenges this week!
