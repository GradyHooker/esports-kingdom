"Field Trip" - It is that time of the week for Valve to drop 6 new challenges on us. It might be the holiday season, but Operation Shattered Web isn't taking any time off. Befriend some chickens, fly with a parachute, and gets kills with a bunch of different weapons.

Shattered Web Week 5 Challenges:

  • Feed the Birds (1 Star): Befriend chickens for 180 seconds in Deathmatch: Defusal Group Delta
  • Scenic Tour (1 Star): Use a parachute for 10 seconds in Danger Zone
  • Stop and Smell the Roses (2 Stars): Get 25 kills with the Negev in Deathmatch: Dust II
  • A Walk on the Breach (2 Stars): Get 10 round wins in Competitive: Breach (Scrimmage)
  • Take the Long Way Home (3 Stars): Get 50 kills in Casual: Defusal Group Sigma
  • Behold the Sites (3 Stars): Get 25 AWP or P90 kills in Guardian: Overpass

    • Good luck in your Shattered Web challenges this week!
