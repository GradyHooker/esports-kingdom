"Money Isn't Everything" - It is Christmas time and Happy Holidays to all, but we have some missions to do! The sixth week of missions for Operation Shattered Web is out and will test us with challenges related to money.

Shattered Web Week 6 Challenges:

  • Penny Pincher (1 Star): Get 2 kills without spending any money in a single Danger Zone match
  • Talk is Cheap (1 Star): Get 20 pistol kills in Deathmatch: Dust II
  • Pocket Change (2 Stars): Get 15 XM1014 kills in Guardian: Bank
  • Early Investor (2 Stars): Get 2 pistol round wins in Competitive (Any Map)
  • Rising Capital (3 Stars): Get 25 kills in Guardian: Vertigo
  • Long Term Profits (3 Stars): Earn $100,000 in Casual: Defusal Group Delta

    • Good luck in your Shattered Web challenges this week!
