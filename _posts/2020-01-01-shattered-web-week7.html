"Hot Shot" - It might be a new year, but it is the same old Counter-Strike. Operation Shattered Web is still ongoing and we have all the week 7 challenges focusing on flashy deagle, headshot, and no-scope kills.

Shattered Web Week 7 Challenges:

  • Pace Yourself (1 Star): Get 10 kills with the Desert Eagle in Casual: Hostage Group
  • They Never Stood a Chance (1 Star): Get 2 sniper rifle kills in Danger Zone
  • That's Not Allowed! (2 Stars): Get 2 unscoped AWP kills in Guardian: Cache
  • How Hard Can It Be? (2 Stars): Get 10 round wins in Competitive: Dust II
  • Coordinated Attack (3 Stars): Get 35 kills in Guardian: Mirage
  • Target Acquired (3 Stars): Get 30 headshot kills in a single Deathmatch: Dust II match

    • Good luck in your Shattered Web challenges this week!
