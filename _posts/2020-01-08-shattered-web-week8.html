"Italian Job" - It is the eighth week of Operation Shattered Web now and we are focusing on a set of maps with similar aesthetics; Canals, Inferno, and Italy.

Shattered Web Week 8 Challenges:

  • Start Your Engines (1 Star): Get 10 kills in Casual: Canals
  • Hit The Gas (1 Star): Get 5 headshot kills in Casual: Inferno
  • Switching Gears (2 Stars): Get 15 kills with CT weapons in Guardian: Italy
  • You're Only Supposed to Blow the Bloody Doors Off! (2 Stars): Get 10 round wins in Competitive: Inferno
  • Rooftop Getaway (3 Stars): Get 16 round wins in Wingman: Rialto
  • A Close Scrape (3 Stars): Get 40 kills with the Dual Berettas in Deathmatch: Defusal Group Delta
