"A Dry Heat" - It is time for everyone to jump into their two favorite maps that look like they are very hot; Mirage and Dust II (including Shortdust). Get some wins in Wingman, kills in Deathmatch, utility damage in Casual and a bunch more.

Shattered Web Week 11 Challenges:

  • No Sweat (1 Star): Get 10 kills with the Tec-9 or Five-Seven in Casual: Dust II
  • Watch Your Fingers (1 Star): Get 20 Breach Charge kills in Guardian: Dust II
  • Preheat Oven to 300 Degrees (2 Stars): Deal 300 utility damage in Casual: Mirage
  • A Hot Desert Wind (2 Stars): Get 10 kills in Danger Zone
  • The Heat Is On (3 Stars): Get 16 round wins in Wingman: Shortdust
  • Scorched Earth (3 Stars): Get 200 kills in Deathmatch: Defusal Group Delta

    • Good luck in your Shattered Web challenges this week!
