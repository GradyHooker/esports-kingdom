"Pump Up The Volume" - It is time to get loud as Valve's next set of missions for Operation Shattered Web focuses on making noise. We have Negev kills, M249 kills, breach charges, and just a casual 200 kills in DeathMatch...amoung others.

Shattered Web Week 12 Challenges:

  • WTB Earplugs (1 Star): Get 5 kills with the Negev in Casual: Dust II
  • Noise Complaints (1 Star): Get 50 M249 kills in Guardian: Inferno
  • Stop Asking About the Train... (2 Stars): Get 10 round wins in Competitive: Overpass
  • Pack a Playlist (2 Stars): Get 40 kills in Arms Race
  • A Loud Alternative (3 Stars): Destroy 5 locked doors with the Breach Charge in Danger Zone
  • CS:GO Starter Pack (3 Stars): Get 200 kills with the AK-47 or M4A4 in Deathmatch: Dust II

    • Good luck in your Shattered Web challenges this week!
