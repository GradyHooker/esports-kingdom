Six months after the conclusion of Worlds 2019, the FunPlus Phoenix skins are finally entering the game in Patch 10.9. The patch also sees buffs for Cho'Gath, Fiddlesticks, Gnar, Kayn, Lissandra, and Quinn as well as nerfs for Bard, Karthus, Nami, Tundle, and Wukong.

For the full list of updates included in League of Legends Patch 10.9 click here.

Skins & Chromas

{% include carousel.html folder="article" images="leagueoflegends-patch-10-9-1|leagueoflegends-patch-10-9-2|leagueoflegends-patch-10-9-3|leagueoflegends-patch-10-9-4|leagueoflegends-patch-10-9-5" captions="FPX Gangplank|FPX Malphite|FPX Vayne|FPX Lee Sin|FPX Thresh" %}

Every year the winning team from the League of Legends Worlds tournament gets to work with Riot to get five custom skins for the champions of their choosing. FunPlus Phoenix won in 2019 and they selected Gangplank, Malphite, Vayne, Lee Sin, and Thresh. The skins are colored in red, gold, and white to resemble "anime-esque super-hero action teams."
