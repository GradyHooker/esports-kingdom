We are roughly halfway into the Summer season for the LCK, LPL, LEC, and LCS. That means it is time for Grady and Ryan to put their heads together and come up with another edition of the Tier Rankings to put the teams where they belong!

PLEASE NOTE: Different regions tiers are not comparable - teams that are in the Europe A-Tier are not necessarily equal to those in the South Korea A-Tier. Each region has been ranked individually and isolated from the others. We have also temporarily removed the S-Tier, reserving that for when the regions collide again and a 'King of League of Legends' can truly be named.

Tier Rankings compiled by Ryan "Hullitude" Hull (@Hulltude on Twitter) and Grady Hooker (@GradyHooker on Twitter).
