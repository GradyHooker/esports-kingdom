It has been 2months since we last looked at which teams are the strongest in their region for Counter-Strike and a lot has happened. Most notably ESL Pro League Season 12 has concluded across the regions!

PLEASE NOTE: Different regions tiers are not comparable - teams that are in the Europe A-Tier are not necessarily equal to those in the Asia-Pacific A-Tier. Each region has been ranked individually and isolated from the others. We have also temporarily removed the S-Tier, reserving that for when the regions collide again and a 'King of Counter-Strike' can truly be named.
