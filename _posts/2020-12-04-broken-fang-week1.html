"Start Your Engines" - It has been a long time since we have had an Operation, but Operation Broken Fang has just dropped and that means we are here to help you get the Diamond Coin with all of the missions.

Broken Fang Week 1 Challenges:

  • Manual Transmission: Win rounds in Competitive: Engage (7 rounds, 15 rounds, 20 rounds)
  • Warming Up: Get AK47, M4A4, M4A1-S kills in Casual: Ancient (7 kills, 15 kills, 25 kills)
  • Ice & Snow, Take It Slow: Get kills in Danger Zone: Frostbite (2 kills, 4 kills, 6 kills)
  • Clutch Control: Win rounds in Retakes (3 rounds, 7 rounds, 10 rounds)
  • Under The Hood (2 stars): The Phoenix have been spotted! Find out what they are planning
  • Mass Production (2 Stars): Get 25 kills in Guardian: Engage
  • Premium Unleaded: Win rounds in Broken Fang Premier (7 rounds, 15 rounds, 20 rounds)

    • Good luck in your Broken Fang challenges this week!
