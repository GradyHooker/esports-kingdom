"Sleight of Hand" - This week it is your turn to do some magic tricks in Counter-Strike. You will be doing classic like the Cup And Balls on Mirage, while also trying to keep some secrets to yourself on Nuke by covering them up with some smoke grenades.

Broken Fang Week 5 Challenges:

  • Cups And Balls: Win rounds in Competitive: Mirage (7 rounds, 15 rounds, 20 rounds)
  • Smoke Show (2 stars): Get 25 kills in Guardian: Mirage
  • Pickpocket: Select an enemy card in Retakes (1 time, 3 times, 5 times)
  • Keep Your Secrets: Detonate smoke grenades at specific locations in Casual: Nuke (5 locations, 10 locations, 15 locations)

    • Good luck in your Broken Fang challenges this week!
