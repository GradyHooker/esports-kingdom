"World Tour" - Are you ready to feel like a rockstar? This week you will be spending your time in Vertigo and Mirage playing your sweet tunes for the players in Wingman, Deathmatch, Demolition, and as always Guardian.

Broken Fang Week 6 Challenges:

  • Rock Is Dead: Win rounds in Wingman: Vertigo (4 rounds, 8 rounds, 12 rounds)
  • Don't Let Me Down (2 stars): Get 20 kills with Terrorist weapons Guardian: Vertigo
  • Backstage Pass: Get kills from Tunnel or Palace in Deathmatch: Mirage (10 kills, 20 kills, 40 kills)
  • Home Sweet Home: Get kills in Demolition (10 kills, 20 kills, 30 kills)

    • Good luck in your Broken Fang challenges this week!
