"Improvise" - I really hope that you love the map Nuke because that is where you are going to be spending a lot of time this week and you will be shooting a lot of SMGs at people in all of Deathmatch, Arms Race, and Guardian!

Broken Fang Week 7 Challenges:

  • No Plan Survives First Contact: Win rounds in Competitive: Nuke (7 rounds, 15 rounds, 20 rounds)
  • When In Doubt, Use More Bullets (2 stars): Get 20 SMG kills in Guardian: Nuke
  • Keep Your Friends Close...: Get SMG kills in Deathmatch: Defusal Group Delta (20 kills, 40 kills, 60 kills)
  • ...And Your Enemies Closer: Get SMG kills in Arms Race (4 kills, 8 kills, 12 kills)

    • Good luck in your Broken Fang challenges this week!
