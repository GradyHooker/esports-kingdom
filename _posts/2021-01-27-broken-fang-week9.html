"Keep Your Eyes Open" - You might be finished with Ancient, but it isn't finished with you as you have one more mission left on that map before you head off to blind people, graffiti on Dust II, and shoot some bots in the head.

Broken Fang Week 9 Challenges:

  • All Warfare Is Based On Deception: Win rounds in Broken Fang Premier (7 rounds, 15 rounds, 20 rounds)
  • Search And Destroy: Win rounds in Competitive: Ancient (7 rounds, 15 rounds, 20 rounds)
  • Evade Detection (2 stars): Get 20 Headshot kills with the AUG in Guardian: Overpass
  • Never Saw It Coming: Blind an enemy with a flashbang in Retakes (1 blinding, 3 blindings, 7 blindings)
  • Deface Off: Apply Graffiti at specific locations in Casual: Dust II (3 graffiti, 7 graffiti, 10 graffiti)
