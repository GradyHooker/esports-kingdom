"One Track Mind" - Choo choo...it is time to visit the map they call Train for all of our missions this week. It is rounds in Competitive, kills on Guardian, headshots in Deathmatch, and heavy weapon kills in Casual - but all on Train.

Broken Fang Week 11 Challenges:

  • Keep On Track: Win rounds in Competitive: Train (7 rounds, 15 rounds, 20 rounds)
  • The Inside Track (2 stars): Get 25 kills in Guardian: Train
  • Tracking Your Location: Get headshot kills in Deathmatch: Train (110 kills, 20 kills, 40 kills)
  • Wrong Side Of The Tracks: Get Heavy weapon kills in Casual: Defusal Group Delta (7 kills, 15 kills, 20 kills)

    • Good luck in your Broken Fang challenges this week!
