Friday the 12 March is when you should tune in to the World of Tanks ANZ twitch channel to see eight teams froma cross New Zealand and Australia represent their city/state for a share of $20,000.

World of Tanks ANZ Product Manager, Jake Truman said about the tournament:

Season two of the ANZ Premier League is set to be bigger and better with new partners, new talent and a prize pool twice as big as season one. We're proud to be continuing the growth of the local esports scene and are keen to see what teams and players have to show us.

Games will be broadcast at https://www.twitch.tv/worldoftanksanz and a full schedule including finals games can be found at https://worldoftanks.asia/en/news/announcements/anzpl-season-two-teams/.

We also know the full slate of partners for the event with Fortress Melbourne as the production partner and both HyperX and Republic of Gamers joining for the season. These partnerships will see gear from both HyperX and Republic of Gamers being given away during the broadcast.

If you can't wait to see the action, then there is a precursor event that will see Bajo and Caaaaate captain teams in a head-to-head battle of the streamers.
