ESL announce the ESL AU&NZ Championship 2018 Season

Grady Hooker (@GradyHooker)

2018.01.10

ESL returns to the Australian and New Zealand scenes in 2018 with two huge seasons expanded to sixteen teams each and branching out into DOTA 2. The CSGO stage will feature a price pool of $20,000 and the online league will be run over seven weeks.

Online Season

The sixteen teams will be split into two groups of eight and will play their opponents in two best-of-ones each matchday. The online season will run for seven weeks starting in early-March and ending in mid-April.

  • Week 1: Wednesday, 7th - Thursday, 8th of March, 2018
  • Week 2: Wednesday, 14th - Thursday, 15th of March, 2018
  • Week 3: Wednesday, 21st - Thursday, 22nd of March, 2018
  • Week 4: Wednesday, 28th - Thursday, 29th of March, 2018
  • Week 5: Wednesday, 5th - Thursday, 6th of April, 2018
  • Week 6: Wednesday, 11th - Thursday, 12th of April, 2018
  • Week 7: Wednesday, 18th - Thursday, 19th of April, 2018

    • LAN Finals

    Out of each group the top four teams will be selected and the top eight will compete in front of an audience to determine the best team in the ANZ region and who takes on the biggest chunk of the $20,000 prize pool

  • Season Finals: June, 2018

    • The Teams & Open Qualifier

    Fourteen teams get a direct invite to the league, while the final two slots are wide open for anyone to qualify for in one of the two open qualifiers. Each open qualifier is a single-elimination best-of-one bracket with a best-of-three final to determine which team advances into the league.

  • Open Qualifier #1: Saturday, 24th of February, 2018
  • Open Qualifier #2: Sunday, 25th of February, 2018
